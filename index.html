Seasons of Sonship Book 1: Foundations

Seasons of Sonship Foundations is a practical "how to" guide for spiritual sons to connect with spiritual fathering leaders to maximize, accelerate, and master their sonship progression towards their inheritance. Written by Joshua Todd, a spiritual son to an international minister, you will get the foundation stones for the remainder of the series to be released in five more consecutive titles.

Seasons of Sonship Book 2: Invitation

The Season of Invitation is the 2nd book in the series, outlining the first season of sonship where a newborn child enters into the pursuit on the pathway of purpose. This season is thrilling! Learn how to embrace the fullest out of the season and correctly navigate relationships as almighty God reveals how He has been orchestrating this divine connection.

Seasons of Sonship Book 3: Interdependence

The Season of Interdependence is the longest season within sonship, as it is where the deep work of Jesus in a discipling relationship is the most revealing about the required preparation of a son or daughter of God to be ready for their inheritance invasion. This season deals with issues such as the fear of man, self-reliance, selfish ambition, haughty pride, infiltrations, and more. The fathering leader works to represent God at the table of trust set before a son or daughter, often partnering with God through strategic communication for that inheritor to reach ultimates. The sounds from invitation of celebration take on a new weighty sound of correction where the LORD will begin to bring correction and release true transformation. The Son or Daughter learns the levels of communication available within fathering, how to tap into the highest level of impartation, and how to embrace the necessary leadership through God's appointed representation for the deep works to occur..

Purified Power: The Power in Prophetic Action

Dr. Joshua Todd currently leads a group of international Prophets in successful and maturing Prophetic Company. Having been mentored on the Prophetic and Apostolic Pathway, there has been a growing interest in the power held within prophetic actions. Purified Power takes a look at healthy aspects and practical application of Prophetic Principles that destroy satan's bondages by appropriating Christ's Victory today. This book is aimed at putting a purified weapon in the hands of Prophetic Leaders who have been called to march as part of Christ's victorious army.
